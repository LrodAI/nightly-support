Your privacy is our priority

Effective Date: February 15, 2026

Last Updated: February 15, 2026

Privacy First: Nightly is designed to keep your habit data private and secure. We collect minimal data and never sell your personal information.

Information We Collect

Habit Data

Nightly stores your habit information locally on your device, including:

  • Habit names and descriptions you create
  • Completion dates and streak counts
  • Custom colors and settings
  • Progress statistics and analytics

Usage Analytics

We may collect anonymous usage data to improve the app:

  • App crashes and performance metrics
  • Feature usage statistics (no personal data)
  • Device type and iOS version (for compatibility)

Subscription Information

If you purchase Premium, Apple processes your payment and shares minimal transaction details with us through RevenueCat for subscription management.

How We Use Your Information

Your Habit Data

  • Local Storage: All habit data stays on your device
  • iCloud Sync: If enabled, data syncs securely through your personal iCloud account
  • No Server Storage: We don't store your habit data on our servers

Analytics Data

  • Improve app performance and fix crashes
  • Understand which features are most helpful
  • Plan new features based on usage patterns

Subscription Data

  • Verify your Premium subscription status
  • Restore purchases when you reinstall the app
  • Provide customer support for billing issues

Data Sharing and Security

We Do NOT Share

  • Your habit names, goals, or completion data
  • Personal information with advertisers
  • Data with social media companies
  • Any information for marketing purposes

Limited Sharing

We only share data with:

  • Apple: For App Store functionality and iCloud sync
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management (anonymized transaction data)
  • Crash Reporting: Anonymous crash logs to fix bugs

Security Measures

  • All data transmission uses encryption
  • No passwords or sensitive personal data collected
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

Your Rights and Controls

Data Control

  • Export: Premium users can export all habit data as CSV
  • Delete: Delete individual habits or all data in Settings
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics in Settings ‚Üí Privacy

Subscription Management

  • Manage subscriptions through your Apple ID settings
  • Cancel anytime - no questions asked
  • Data remains accessible even after cancellation

Account Deletion

To completely remove your data:

  1. Delete the Nightly app from your device
  2. Turn off iCloud sync for Nightly (if previously enabled)
  3. Contact us if you need help with subscription cancellation

Children's Privacy

Nightly is rated 4+ and safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you're a parent and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. When we do:

  • We'll update the "Last Updated" date at the top
  • Significant changes will be announced in the app
  • Your continued use constitutes acceptance of changes

International Users

Nightly is available worldwide. Your data is processed according to this Privacy Policy regardless of your location. If you're in the EU, you have additional rights under GDPR - contact us for details.

Contact Us

Questions about privacy? We're here to help:

  • Email: privacy@nightly.app
  • Support: support@nightly.app
  • Response Time: Within 48 hours for privacy inquiries
Bottom Line: Your habit-building journey is personal. We built Nightly to respect your privacy while helping you build better habits. Your data stays yours.
