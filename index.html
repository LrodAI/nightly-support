How do I create my first habit?
Tap the "+" button on your main screen to add a new habit. Give it a name, choose a color, and set up reminders if you'd like. Start with just 1-2 habits for the best results.
What is the constellation view?
Your constellation is a beautiful visualization of your progress. Each habit completion creates a star, and consistent streaks form connecting patterns. Watch your personal constellation grow as you build better habits!
How do streaks work?
Complete a habit each day to build your streak. If you miss a day, your streak resets, but don't worry - every new day is a fresh start. Focus on consistency over perfection.

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Can I track unlimited habits?
Free users can track up to 3 habits. Premium users get unlimited habit tracking to build comprehensive routines.
Is my data private?
Absolutely. Your habit data stays on your device and in your private cloud backup. We don't share or sell your personal information.
How do I restore my subscription?
Go to Settings ‚Üí Subscription ‚Üí Restore Purchases. This will restore any previous Premium purchases tied to your Apple ID.
Can I export my data?
Premium users can export their habit data as CSV files for analysis in spreadsheet apps or for backup purposes.
What if I miss a day?
Don't worry! Missing one day doesn't ruin your progress. Your streak will reset, but all your previous completions are still tracked. Just start again tomorrow.
